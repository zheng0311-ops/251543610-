The Chinese knot is created by handicraftmen at the beginning.Through hundreds of improvement,it has already become an elegant and colorful arts and the crafts.In ancient times,people used it to record events.But now,it is mainly aimed at docoration.
"Knot"means love,marriage and reunion in Chinese.The Chinese knot is often exchanged as a gift or used as a docoration to pray for good luck and drive out evil spirits.This handicraft has been passed down from generation to generation.Now,it has been more and more popular both in China and around the world.
